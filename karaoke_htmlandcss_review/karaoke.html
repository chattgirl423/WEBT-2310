Karaoke


Our karaoke restaurant offers a variety of private karaoke rooms, each designed to enhance your singing experience.


**The VIP Lounge** is our largest room, perfect for groups of up to 20, with premium sound systems, custom lighting, and luxurious seating.
For smaller gatherings, **The Retro Room** accommodates up to 10 guests and features a nostalgic 80s vibe with retro decor and classic hits. **The Party Pod** is a cozy space for up to 6 people, ideal for intimate karaoke sessions with friends.
All rooms come equipped with high-quality microphones, touchscreen song selectors, and personalized service, ensuring an unforgettable night of music and fun.
